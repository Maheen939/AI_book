## Research Strategy

### 1. Peer-Reviewed Sources (Target: 10-12 sources)

#### Search Databases & Keywords

**IEEE Xplore** (www.ieeexplore.ieee.org)
- Keywords: "humanoid robotics", "robot control systems", "AI robotics integration"
- Filter: Last 10 years, peer-reviewed
- Target: 4-5 papers

**ACM Digital Library** (dl.acm.org)
- Keywords: "humanoid robots", "robot learning", "embodied AI"
- Filter: Peer-reviewed, relevance to education/physical AI
- Target: 2-3 papers

**arXiv** (arxiv.org)
- Keywords: "humanoid robotics", "physical AI", "robot kinematics"
- Filter: cs.RO (Robotics) category, papers with institutional affiliation
- Target: 2-3 papers

**Google Scholar** (scholar.google.com)
- Keywords: "Physical AI", "humanoid robot design", "AI in physical systems"
- Filter: Cited references (higher credibility)
- Target: 2-3 papers

**Other Databases**
- ResearchGate (researcher profiles)
- SSRN (pre-prints and working papers)
- Conference proceedings (ICRA, IROS, RSS)

## Reference Quality Checklist

For each source, verify:
- [ ] Publication date within last 10 years (prefer last 5)
- [ ] Author credentials and institutional affiliation
- [ ] Relevance to Physical AI, humanoid robotics, or AI integration
- [ ] DOI or persistent URL available
- [ ] Full text accessible or summary sufficient
- [ ] Properly formatted in APA 7th Edition
